About Us

FURAIKA FOUNDATION -

Is a not- for- profit organisation founded in Australia that is committed into helping vulnerable children and their guardians in need, by raising funds that will provide supporting services and training programs to children in Mombasa Kenya through our Organisation known as Furaika center. This funds will help improve the future lives of the children and their guardians. We kindly ask for you to support us to help these children and and their guardians in need.

Furaika is a Kenyan word that means to be Joyful.

Our objective is to raise funds that will help to provide services to the Furaika center in Mombasa, Kenya. That will elevate the dreams of vulnerable children in need by offering support and educational programs for both the children and their guardians. Furaika center is a not-for-profit organisation in Mombasa that supports, care and educational programs to vulnerable children, their guardians and HIV affected women who are struggling to meet their basic needs for their day to day life This programs includes:
  • Education program
  • Hygiene programs
  • Health programs
  • Creating environmental awareness
  • In proving ways to access clean water
  • Environmental hygiene programs
  • Skills nurturing programs
  • Good communication programs
  • Community emporwerment programs
  • Leveraged local family support from our local communities.
  • Entrepreneurship programs
  • Technology programs
  • Empowering children and single mother -

    • Our vision

    Is to expand locally and overseas and eradicate poverty around the world.

    Our Mission

    Is to provide protective care for vulnerable children and their guardians who are in need, and to work hand in hand with the local churches, ministries, communities and schools by providing programs for orphans in various activities that respects the integrity of the local community and creates long term positive change for the community at large.

    Our Objective :

    Help and create hope for vulnerable women and their children in crisis poverty circumstances in Kenya.

    Goals :

    A community that offers a loving caring environment for our children by supporting their physical, emotional and mental well-being by working together with the local bodies to develop Educational structures around them for a better World.
